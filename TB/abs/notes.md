## Introduction

ABS stands for Auto Buy Sell System, which is a trading system that automates the buying and selling of assets based on predefined strategies.developed by Jay patel

## How ABS Works

ABS uses algorithms to analyze market data and make trades according to the rules. 


## Key Components of ABS

1. **Strategy**: The set of rules that define when to buy or sell an asset.
2. **Data Feed**: The source of market data used to analyze and make trades.
3. **Broker API**: The interface that connects the ABS to the brokerage platform.
4. **Risk Management**: The system that controls the amount of capital at risk.
